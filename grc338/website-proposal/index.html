Introduction

Elizabeth Boutique

Elizabeth is a boutique clothing company with one in person location and online shopping options. The company sells tops, bottoms, and dresses.

Target audience

The target audience for this store is young women or woman identifying/feminine dressing people. These people are likely to be fairly tech savvy.

There would be two reasons that people would visit this website, to make a purchase or to find out more about the instore location. The website would have the main function of allowing customers to look for and buy clothing online. The website would also need to include the information for the instore location for customers looking for this information.
